About iCarpe Diem

From Latin to English, the phrase "carpe diem" roughly translates to "seize the day." Here at iCarpe Diem, we want to encourage our members to not only seize the day, but to also seize their lives. Each individual has dreams and aspirations worth pursuing and we want to to help our members in their journeys toward achieving their goals. This application will allow you to create a bucket list. A bucket list is simply a list of things that you want to accomplish before you die. You will categorize the items and mark their statuses. This will allow you to track how you are doing toward achieving your stated goals. You can add and delete items from your list at any time. The first step toward achieving your goals is to define them and then commit. If you are not yet a member, begin that process and sign up and then sign in today! If you are a member, welcome back! You may sign in and continue your journey!
