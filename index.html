About iCarpe Diem

We want to encourage our members to not only seize the day but to also seize their life. Each individual has dreams and aspirations worth pursuing and we want to to encourage them in their journey toward achieving their goals. This application will allow you to create a bucketlist of items that you want to complete before you die. You will then categorize the items and mark their status. This will allow you to track how you are doing toward achieving your stated goals. You can add and delete items from your list at any time. But the first step toward achieving your goals is to define them and then commit. If you are not yet a member, begin that process and join today! If you are a member, welcome back! You may sign in and continue your journey!
